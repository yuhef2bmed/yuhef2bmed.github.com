Green Tips:
For Parents!

TIP!
Planning for a family outing? Consider
bringing your children out to the park for a
lovely, peaceful picnic. Pack simple lunches and snacks,
then spend the rest of your day with nature!

TIP!
Getting a new car, or planning to
repaint your current one? Consider painting it a
bright white. This prevents it from overheating
on hot days, causing it to use less energy!

TIP!
Work with your kids to help create their very own
garden. This helps build their knowledge on plant life,
and encourages them to appreciate nature as well.
Not to mention it can be a lot of fun too!
