Our Story

'Go Green Kids' is an independent initiative that aims to educate the future generation of the importance of conserving the environment. Created in 2014, we are a new but safe website, suitable for kids 5-13 years of age.

As we are independent, we ask only for your co-operation by letting kids know about our website, as well as to guide them through our fun activities if need be. If you have any further enquiries, please visit our Contact Us page.
