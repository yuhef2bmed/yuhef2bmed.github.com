Green Tips:
For Kids!

TIP!
Check, check, and triple check
that you turn off your lights
when you're not using them!

Aw man, who left the lights on?
Click on them to turn them off!

TIP!
When going to bed,
use fans instead of
air-conditioners, or aircons!

Fans use less energy than aircons,
and they are mighty strong too!

TIP!
Rather than using a shower when you bathe,
use a big pail of water instead!

Brr! It's cold, but using pails can
save a lot of fresh water!
